Module 4: Protecting Inference with F5 AI Guardrails
====================================================

In all the labs we've done today, we're working from the assumption that everything with AI tooling is all puppies and
rainbows and of course it's all secure, right? Of course not, with great tools comes great responsibility, and people
and workloads need to be protected. In this lab, we'll introduce `F5 AI Guardrails <https://www.f5.com/products/ai-guardrails#overview>`__ protections, which can in out-of-band
mode like in this lab, protect even your local AI inferencing.
